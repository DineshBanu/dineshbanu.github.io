About Us..

Home / About Us

Let us introduce ourselves as one of the leading service providers for all textile electrical and electronic products for a spinning mill. We extend our services to all let out products of premier and Uster.

We support customers where they come across stumbling blocks like poor service support and reprehensible diagnosis of parts that in turn may result in undue expenditures, huge service charges and not giving any guidance on their products etc. These types of services we manage with a team consisting of ex-service engineers, having gained excellent work experience from OEM suppliers.

Moreover, we do not compromise on the parts that we replace in the modules, as we seek the same source of supply. Our core objective of service is to make the equipment work at minimum cost with quality service and less downtime. This we make happen with our trained team.

We carry out our services in North India through M/s Trishul overseas, western India through Vijayashree Texcoms, Eastern India and Bangladesh through RSM service traders situated at Dhaka. We provide services either on call basis or on contract basis, which customers can opt for.
